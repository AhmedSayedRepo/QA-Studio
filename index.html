Get started

Up and running in about a minute

1

Download install.bat

One small file — no ZIP to unzip, nothing to configure.

2

Double-click it

It installs Python if needed, pulls the app, adds dependencies, and drops a Desktop icon.

3

Open QA Studio

Launch from the new Desktop icon, add your keys in Setup, and run.

Titles or full steps

Generate concise test-case titles, or complete step-by-step cases — your choice per run.

English & Arabic

Produce test content in either language with a single toggle in Setup.

Writes back to Azure DevOps

Pulls your projects, test plans & stories and pushes generated cases straight into them.

Your choice of model

Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, NVIDIA, Azure OpenAI or Ollama — bring your own key.

!

A couple of things to know. Windows may show a blue “Windows protected your PC” notice for the .bat — click More info → Run anyway. If it says Python was just installed, close the window and run install.bat once more. Your API keys & Azure PAT are stored locally only and never uploaded.
